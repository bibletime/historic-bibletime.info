<H3>i18n("Installation of Sword's text modules")</H3>

<P>
i18n("This page explains how to add the text modules, which are available from Crosswire, to the BibleTime software to make it really useful.")
</P>

<P>
i18n("The modules are available as ZIP files from the Crosswire Bible society. Have a look into the &raquo;Modules&laquo; section of the BibleTime web pages
to get an overview of the available modules.")
i18n("Before you start installing modules please make sure BibleTime is installed!")<BR>
</P>

<P>
i18n("First you have to find the directory, which we'll call now &raquo;Sword directory&laquo;, on your system. The Sword directory contains all Sword data,
i.e. it contains at least the sub-directories &raquo;modules/&laquo; and &raquo;mods.d/&laquo;.")
i18n("Then you have to unzip the downloaded ZIP file(s) into the Sword directory to populate the two subdirectories we mentioned.")
</P>

<p>
i18n("After restarting BibleTime the modules should appear in the main index on the left side of the main window.")
</p>

<P>
i18n("The installation instructions made by the Sword team are available here:")
<A HREF="http://www.crosswire.org/sword/modules/moduleinstall.jsp" TARGET="_blank">www.crosswire.org</A>.<BR>
i18n("If you get into troubles please read the answers to the frequently asked questions. The FAQ is available on the left side of this page.")
</P>
